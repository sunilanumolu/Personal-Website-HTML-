SUNDAR PICHAI

Pichai Sundararajan (born 12 July 1972), also known as Sundar Pichai, is an Indian American business executive. Pichai is the chief executive officer (CEO) of Google Inc.Formerly the Product Chief of Google, Pichai's current role was announced on 10 August 2015, as part of the restructuring process that made Alphabet Inc. into Google's parent company,and he assumed the position on 2 October 2015. Pichai worked in engineering and product management at Applied Materials and in management consulting at McKinsey and Company. Pichai is married to Anjali Pichai and has two children.Pichai's interests include soccer and cricket. He is an avid fan of FC Barcelona and states that "he watches every game of the club". Pichai had been suggested as a contender for Microsoft's CEO in 2014, a position that was eventually given to Satya Nadella.
